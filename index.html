About Me

Hello! I've got over 30 years in software development, developing applications supporting diverse goals such as personnel distribution and assignment, magazine circulation fulfillment, help desk issues tracking and administration, sales force hierarchy and commission tracking, and most recently, cable media advertising sales.

I'm originally from south Jersey (Go Eagles! Go Phillies!). I received a BA in computer science from Rutgers University, and served in the US Navy. I spent 3 years on a ship stationed in Rhode Island, and finished my service doing IT work in enlisted personnel assignment in northern Virginia. For the last twenty years I've supported domestic advertising sales for Discovery Communications. In the '90s I played drums and guitar in a band here in DC called Racecar. I'm the proud father of two great kids, Eva and Owen; my wife and I live with them in Silver Spring, MD with our dog, Mabel. In my free time I enjoy playing guitar (poorly), reading, watching TV, and sneaking in a few minutes on my son's Xbox.
